surf browser cheatsheet

options
-b 	disable scrollbars
-B 	enable scrollbars
-d	disable disk cache
-D
-f	disable fullscreen (use windowed mode)
-F
-g	disable geolocation
-G
-i	disable images
-I
-s	disable javascript
-S
-z 80	specify zoomlevel

key binding
^ h	back in history
^ L	forward in history
^ j	scroll down
^ k	scroll up
esc	stop loading
^ b	back one page
^ spc	down one page
^ u	scroll left
^ i	scroll right
^ + 	zoom in (also ^ K)
^ -	zoom out (also ^ J)
^ Q	reset zoom
^ /	search
^ n	next search result
^ N
^ g	URL bar (requires dmenu)
^ p	load URI from selection
^ P	print
^ r 	reload
^ R 	reload w/o cache
^ y	yank URI
^ B	toggle scroll bars
^ I	toggle image auto-load
^ S	toggle javascript execution
F11 	toggle fullscreen
^ A	toggle cookie policies (no reload)

Indicators
A	All cookies acceptes
a	No cookies
@	All escept third parties
g G	Geolocation
d D	Disk cache
i I	Images
s S	Scripts
m M	styles
